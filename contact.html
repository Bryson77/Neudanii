— Common Questions

What types of clothing can you customise?

Anything you wear. Jackets, jeans, coats, shirts, hoodies, trousers, accessories, shoes — if it exists as clothing, we can customise it. We also build pieces entirely from scratch.

Do you use sustainable materials?

Yes — always where possible. We actively source organic cotton, recycled fabrics, reclaimed denim, deadstock silks, and repurposed leather. We'll always tell you exactly what's going into your piece.

How much does a commission cost?

Every piece is priced individually based on materials, complexity, and time. We'll discuss pricing openly during your free consultation — no surprises, ever.

Will my piece ever be duplicated?

Never. Every Neudanii piece comes with a numbered certificate of authenticity. Your design, your colourways, your exact spec — it will never be replicated.

Can I send in my own garment to be reworked?

Absolutely. Reworks and customisations on pieces you already own are welcome — and often produce the most personal results.
